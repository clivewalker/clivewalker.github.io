Introduction

Hi folks

It's been really interesting for me to see the Perch UI Pattern Library in this issue. And it's inspired me to re-assess how I create my own sites. Even though I'm sure my sites are less demanding than the Perch admin UI, I think I would definitely benefit from a pattern library/style guide approach.

In other news, it's been almost a year since the first issue of Perchology on 20th July 2016. Many thanks to everyone who has supported the newsletter in that time.
