Your Voice Wall

A Perch site I created in 2020 that I've been proud to be a part of is Your Voice Wall. It shows posts by people with learning difficulties, expressing themselves with words, pictures and art, telling of how they have struggled and grown during the pandemic. They are truly amazing. I've been in tears at times as I see their resilience, talents and creativity.
